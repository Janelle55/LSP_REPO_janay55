My perspective on using AI in programming has not really changed over the semester because I have always believed that AI is meant to make things easier, not harder. It helps with understanding concepts, recalling information, and doing research quickly, even though it can sometimes be inaccurate. I believe that the idea that AI makes programmers lazy depends on the programmer, not the tool itself. If used properly, AI can help us learn new concepts that we may not have encountered before and make learning more accessible at all times.
